What are five different JAVA data types? What are they used for?

The five different data types are:
	int - short for integer, used for whole number, no decimals        Ex.) -4, 2
	float - 32 bit, used for decimals
	short, long, double - (short)=16 bit, (long)=64 bit, (doubles)=64 bit - used for decimals numbers
	Char - used for characters         Ex.) "b"; "1"; "+"
	string - used for a collection of chars "wpo48utqw[3"
	boolean - used for true or false, on and off
